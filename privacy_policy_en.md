# Privacy Policy - Almosul Shop

**Last Updated:** December 1, 2025

## Introduction

At "Almosul Shop", we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- **Full Name**: To create your account and personalize your experience
- **Phone Number**: To contact you regarding orders
- **Email Address**: To send updates and notifications
- **Profile Picture**: (Optional) To customize your account

### 2. Location Information
We use location services for the following purposes:
- **Show Nearby Stores**: To display stores and malls closest to you
- **Delivery Address**: To facilitate the delivery process
- **Service Improvement**: To show local offers suitable for your location

> **Note:** You can control location permissions from your device settings at any time.

### 3. Usage Information
- **Order History**: To track your orders and improve service
- **Favorite Products**: To save your interests
- **Search History**: To improve search results

### 4. Device Information
- **Device Type and OS**: To ensure app compatibility
- **Unique Device Identifier**: To send notifications
- **IP Address**: For security and fraud prevention

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

1. **Service Delivery**: Process orders and deliveries
2. **Experience Enhancement**: Personalize content and offers
3. **Communication**: Send order updates and special offers
4. **Security**: Protect your account and prevent fraud
5. **Analytics**: Understand app usage to improve it
